Where all the colors and art coming to life begins! Colors are a great way to convey a message to your fellow audience.

Color Wheels

These are a set of colors in a specific order!

This a standard Color wheel

Hue/Color

Are the absolute colors found in any Color Wheel!

Possible Colors

Different Color Wheels

These are the types commonly found around you! But we are mostly focusing on RYB Color spectrum for easier understanding

RGB (Red, Green, Blue)

Most accurate color wheel, It is most vibrant and are commonly seen to the visibile spectrum of humanas!

RYB (Red, Yellow, Blue)

Is a traditional color wheel where it isnt accurate and can sometimes cannot create certain colors

CMYK (Cyan, Magenta, Yellow)

Color wheel with duller colors but tends to create and have higher ranges of color to use from

Note: CMYK Also contains Black!

Different Color Schemes

These are the types commonly used for painting and/or rendering.

Analogous Color

Contains the 3 colors that are directly next to each other!

Monochromatic Color

Consists of only 1 color differing it with a darker and lighter hue

Triadic Colors

Creats an equalatiral shape on the color wheel

Complimentary Color

Colors that are directly Across the color wheel!

Split Complimentary Color

Creates a Y section inside the color wheel

Value

Usually how you use dark and light colors for your drawing!

Lightness Adds Softness

Darkness Adds Richness

Saturation

Brightness/Dullness of your colors!

Brightness Adds High Energy

Dullness creates a calm Energy

Hue

This Depends on your Saturation and Value and cannot be in the negative or positive connotation !
